About

Hi — I’m Addie Swanner, the photographer behind Swan Photo. For the past eight years in Portland I’ve been waking up for golden light and paying attention to the quiet, honest moments that become memories. My goal is to create images that feel warm, real, and timeless.

I work with a simple kit and a calm approach so people can relax and be themselves. Whether it’s a forest wedding, a cozy couples session, or graduation portraits in the city, I bring gentle direction, clear communication, and thoughtful packages. I’m also available to travel with compensation.

Favorites:
  • Golden hour portraits deep in forest light
  • Quiet ceremony details, natural emotion first
  • Urban grunge + soft romantic gradients
